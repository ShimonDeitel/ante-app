The deal

Three cards. One morning.

No streak freezes, no forgiveness tokens, no motivational quotes. Just your own money, sitting on the table until the bed is made.

1

Set the stakes

Pick your wake time and your ante: one dollar to one thousand. Snoozing gets its own price tag. You choose every number before the night begins.

2

Prove the bed

When the alarm rings, make your bed and photograph it. Ante compares it to your reference photo entirely on your device. Match it, and the alarm settles for free.

3

Or pay the ante

Kill the alarm without the photo and the ante is owed. The app collects before it does anything else. Skipping stops being free, and mornings stop being negotiable.

The app

A casino table for your morning.

Deep felt green, brass gold, and a chip stack that grows with your nerve. Built native for iPhone on iOS 26.

Alarms that break through silent modeBuilt on Apple's AlarmKit, the same machinery as the system clock. Full-screen alert, Dynamic Island countdown, no notification to swipe away.
Photos never leave your phoneThe bed check runs on-device with Apple's Vision framework. Nothing is uploaded, ever. Your bedroom stays yours.
Every dollar trackedStreaks, mornings kept, and a running total of everything you have forfeited, designed to be a number you want to keep small.
